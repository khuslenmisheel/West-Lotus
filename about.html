Our Mission

Culture

West Lotus is a clothing brand born from the heart of the immigrant experience, blending the deep cultural roots of Asia with the freedom and diversity of America. Founded by Asian American students, the brand stands as a tribute to the journey of balancing two worlds: staying connected to the traditions of home while embracing the new opportunities that America offers.
